## App Name

## Short Description (80 chars max)

## Full Description (4000 chars max)
YourJannah connects you to your local mosque like never before.

PRAYER TIMES
- Accurate prayer times with countdown to next salah
- Leave-by reminders with travel time + wudhu buffer
- Walk/drive directions to your mosque
- Jumu'ah times with khutbah schedule

EVENTS & CLASSES
- Browse upcoming events at your mosque
- Filter by category: talks, sports, youth, sisters, community
- RSVP free or buy tickets
- Enrol in classes (Quran, Arabic, business skills)

DONATE & FUNDRAISE
- Quick donate with preset amounts
- Every Friday, monthly, or one-off donations
- Support fundraising campaigns with progress tracking
- Become a monthly patron (£5/£10/£20)
- 100% reaches your masjid — zero platform fees

BUSINESS DIRECTORY
- Find trusted local professionals (solicitors, plumbers, tutors)
- Discover masjid sponsors and community businesses
- Search by category or service type

COMMUNITY
- Check in at your mosque and earn points
- Leaderboard for your mosque community
- Push notifications for prayer reminders and announcements
- Subscribe to multiple mosques

MOSQUE FEATURES
- Book rooms and masjid services (nikkah, funeral, counselling)
- View live streams and archived recordings
- Contact your mosque directly

Download YourJannah and connect with your mosque community today.
